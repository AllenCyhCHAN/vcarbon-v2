About Us

Who We Are

A team of passionate professionals, rooted from the community for the community

V Carbon ESG Group (“V Carbon”) is a solution provider for the development of Environmental Protection, Social Responsibility, and Sustainability in PNG. We chartered 4 different REDD+ projects, under 4 subsidiaries targeting to register with VERRA in late 2023 Total projects account for approximately 1.1 million hectares with an estimated 150 million units of carbon credits over the coming 30 years. These projects could easily be one of the largest projects of its kind in the Asia-Pacific region.

The preservation of these forests is essential not only to the biodiversity but also to the well-being and prosperity of the local community people. This allows the landowners, from one of the least developed rural areas, to actively participate in socio-economic development activities on their motherland, such as creating employment to to generate income and improve rural quality of life living standards, providing new agricultural technology skills, and training opportunities enhancing subsistence farming or larger scale commercial farming, and many more. V Carbon has already been working closely with the PNG Government to apply the forestry projects as for the application of the United Nations REDD+ program.

What we stand for: “Together, we are greener”.

Our mission is to conserve the ultimate green resources in PNG and to find strategies to enhance and merge the green resources into the world with an ESG-focused objective. We aim to transform PNG into a truly sustainable and self-financing green community, behind our professionals, dedications, and relationships we have had built and are going to develop.

In the initial stage of our social development projects, we aim to cover 6 of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (“UNSDG”), namely No Poverty, Decent Work and Economic Growth, Sustainable Cities and Communities, Industry Innovation and Infrastructure, Climate Action and Life on Land. Then in the subsequent stages, we will be tackling the other UNSDGs including Gender Equality, Reduced Inequalities, Good Health and Well Being, Quality Education, Clean Water and Sanitation, and Affordable and Clean Energy.

What’s our history: A transformation story, rooted from the care of the community

Our history goes way back to early 2000’s, when only a few companies were exploring the commercial logging business opportunities in PNG.

V Carbon has started identifying business opportunities in PNG since 2006, and subsequently took over the land use rights from these logging companies and converted the land use rights into forest preservation.

V Carbon, with its professional parties, launched an awareness trip in PNG in August 2023 to lay the foundations for the many projects that are in the pipeline. We also had the opportunity to speak with the local government representatives, landowner representatives, and Incorporated Land Group (“ILG”) representatives to gain their trust and support.

What we do: “Teach a man to fish, you feed him for a lifetime”

V Carbon’s primary focus is the preservation of the current green landscape in PNG.

Forming strong partnership with the landlords and the locals, V Carbon will be launching a variety of social development projects with the short-term goal of improving the livelihoods of the locals, leveraging on the wealth of natural resources. The mid to long term goals are to help the locals to become self-sustainable and to connect to the developed economies. Give a man a fish, you feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish, you feed him for a lifetime.

With nearly 20 years of operational experience in PNG, V Carbon is the perfect road map for interested project developers and investors and also a trusted partner for the locals.

V Carbon, together with its professional parties, traveled to PNG during the final week of in August 2023 for a site visit of the 4 projects; where data sampling and village visiting have been done for the 4 project sites for its implementation of REDD+ initiatives and VCM Projects.
